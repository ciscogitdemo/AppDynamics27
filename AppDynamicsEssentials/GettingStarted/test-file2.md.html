Self Service Trial

The default trial configuration is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) deployment hosted by AppDynamics. 

  1. Follow this Start Free Trial link.
  2. Enter your information and create a secure password.

    1. The email and password you enter will be your login throughout your AppDynamics trial experience. Therefore, it is essential that you use a valid email address.

      You can skip the form and chat with a live expert by using the online chat feature.

  3. Agree to the terms and click Create an Account.
  4. Click Launch AppDynamics.
  • A Controller Tenant for data collection.
  • A Controller Tenant UI for data analysis.
  • An online Account Management Portal to manage your account, users, subscriptions, license usage, and role functionality.
